To my person, my place, my other half

It does feel surreal that we have been doing this for seven years, loving each other across seas then, and now across continents. Not a day passes without me wondering how fate could have been written in such a way that it almost teases its readers. From avoiding love to knitting a forever vow. You flipped my world over.

I might act like I'm used to watching you achieve your dreams one big step at a time, but it never truly stops me from falling into awe over and over again. It always brings me back to the very first reason I was so sure of you. As an individual, you inspire me. As a woman, you make me want to follow your lead.

I hope this year brings you peace in your heart, success in everything you work for, good health, and happiness that finds you in the smallest moments too. And selfishly, I hope this is one step closer to the day we no longer have to count hours apart or say goodbye through a screen.

— forever yours, Adek ♡
